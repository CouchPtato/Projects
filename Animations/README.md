# 🌌 AstroWeb

Welcome to **AstroWeb**, your portal to the universe—right from your browser!  
Whether you’re an aspiring astronomer, a stargazing enthusiast, or just curious about the cosmos, AstroWeb is designed to ignite your curiosity about the night sky.

---

## 🚀 What is AstroWeb?

AstroWeb is a web-based platform for exploring, visualizing, and learning about the wonders of astronomy. Think of it as your digital observatory: modular, extendable, and ready to grow as fast as your fascination with the stars.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Interactive Astronomy Tools**: Visualize celestial events, track planets, or simulate cosmic phenomena (future goals!).
- **Educational Content**: Learn about constellations, stars, planets, and more—right in your browser.
- **Modular Design**: Easy to extend with your own astronomy tools, data, or visualizations.
- **Beautiful UI**: Designed to make space exploration fun and accessible.
